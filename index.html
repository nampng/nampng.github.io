Language Learning

Language learning is a great hobby since it pushes you to learn everyday and exposes you to different cultures.

The learning method I use is called MIA or Mass Immersion Approach. It's the idea that language learning is mainly input, such as listening and reading, and that constant immersion and consumption of native media will help you achieve fluency.
